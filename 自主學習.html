Neural network

\[w_1x_1 + w_2x_2 + \cdots + w_nx_n + b = y\] Where w are the weights and x are the inputs of the neural network function.
Activation function: \[A(y) = z\]

Forward propagation

\[ \begin{aligned} \mathbf{Z}^{[l]} &= \mathbf{W}^{[l]} \mathbf{A}^{[l-1]} + \mathbf{b}^{[l]} \\ \mathbf{A}^{[l]} &= g^{[l]}(\mathbf{Z}^{[l]}), \quad \text{for } l = 1, 2, \dots, L \end{aligned} \] \[ \mathbf{X} = \mathbf{A}^{[0]} = \begin{bmatrix} x_{11} & x_{12} \\ x_{21} & x_{22} \\ x_{31} & x_{32} \end{bmatrix}, \quad \mathbf{W}^{[1]} = \begin{bmatrix} w_{11} & w_{12} & w_{13} \\ w_{21} & w_{22} & w_{23} \\ w_{31} & w_{32} & w_{33} \\ w_{41} & w_{42} & w_{43} \end{bmatrix}, \quad \mathbf{b}^{[1]} = \begin{bmatrix} b_1 \\ b_2 \\ b_3 \\ b_4 \end{bmatrix} \]
